This is a quickly developed page to get data from the MPCs NEOCP records and convert it to normal OBS80 frmat.

Enter the temporary designation of the NEOCP in the textbox. Then press Fetch.

the raw NEOCP data will appear in the upper text area and the OBS80 version in the lower text area.

The Obs80 version will also be placed in the clipboard ready for use in Find_Orb.

Enter NEOCP is:

NEOCP Data

80-Col Observations




When the results appear, they will be on you clipboard.
