ADES to Obs80

This page will convert Astrometrica ADES output to the old 80-column format. This may sometimes be necessary when submitting observations to tools (like MPChecker) that do not accept ADES. Note there is an inherrent loss of precision when converting to Obs80 and you should submit raw ADES data to the MPC when making reports.

Copy the entire content of the Astrometrica ADES.psv file and paste it into the ADES Report text area below. Then press Convert.

The 80-column format will appear in the lower text area and is in the clipboard ready to paste into an observation file.

The conversion does not handle designation conversions for comet fragments, satellites or old survey designations.
